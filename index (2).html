Mental Health is a real world issue

A lot of people are under the wrong impression that mental health isn't a significant issue and doesn't deserve people's attention. But in recent years we've seen death rates due to mental health related issue increase more than physical accidents or conditions between the age group of 13-59

Lack of financial resources, unreasonable parental and curricular expectations, lack of moral support and just overall neglegience remain some of the main causes for suicides by students during the pandemic period.
